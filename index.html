Our Story

Golden Pineapple is where you can find exquisite southern Asian cuisine. You can count on us for a fantastic meal. We never let our customers down because Malaysian cuisine is our specialty. When the customer understands what we have to offer in healthy food, you can rest easy knowing that you made the right choice. Our combination of menu, price, and service can't be beat. You won't find better value in vegetarian dishes anywhere else. We let you know everything about our menu, and when the customer is informed you always get the best deal possible. Customers expect nothing less than the best and we always deliver on that promise. It's our mission to provide complete customer satisfaction in catering. Just let us know what you like and we'll make it for you. You won't find a better Asian fusion restaurant in Fremont, CA. We look forward to seeing you soon!
